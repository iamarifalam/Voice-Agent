title: Order Tracking Policy
topic: order-status

Customers can request order status using their order number, email address, or registered phone number. Orders move through four stages: confirmed, packed, dispatched, and delivered. If an order remains in packed state for more than forty eight hours, it should be escalated to fulfillment support.

When delivery is delayed beyond the promised date, agents should apologize, confirm the latest scan event, and offer a callback if carrier updates remain unavailable.
